	Description:
		Abstract:
			The 1 to 500,000 scale Topographic Base Map series are
			digital maps that show lakes, rivers, streams, railway lines,
			city limits, towns and villages in Manitoba. Additional
			features such as township, range, first nations lands, parks,
			and longitude / latitude lines are also shown.
		Purpose:
			To provide the public with medium resolution topographic
			base map coverage of Manitoba.
